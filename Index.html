About Us

At CBCIVIL, we are a multidisciplinary civil construction company dedicated to delivering high-quality solutions in the construction of storm-water drainage systems, sewer systems, and water main system. Our expertise extends to both subdivisions and already developed areas, ensuring effective and sustainable drainage solutions tailored to the specific needs of each project. Our experienced team is committed to excellence and innovation, working diligently to meet the unique challenges of civil hydraulics and drainage. We also specialize in underground services, including the installation of electrical conduits and setting pits, providing comprehensive infrastructure solutions that enhance community development. At CBCIVIL, we prioritize safety, environmental responsibility, and customer satisfaction. We believe in building lasting relationships with our clients and partners, collaborating closely to achieve shared goals. Choose CBCIVIL for your civil construction needs, and experience the difference of working with a dedicated team that values quality and integrity in every aspect of our work.
